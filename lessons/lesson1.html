Lesson 1 - Music Notes

Learn what music notes are
and how to read them.

Image


Explanation

A music note is a symbol that represents a specific sound's pitch.
A note is what represents all modern written music.
A music note comes in many different shapes and sizes.
But all music notes are represented by a letter.


Each note is represented by the letters
A - B - C - D - E - F - G
